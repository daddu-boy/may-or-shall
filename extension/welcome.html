May or Shall

You're one step from clipping.

This clipper saves highlights from any web page into your May or Shall account, as typed, source-linked cards. Connect it to your account once and you're set.

Open May or Shall & sign in →
  1. Sign in with your email (we send you a one-click link — no password).
  2. Go to Settings → API tokens and click Create token. Copy it.
  3. Click the May or Shall icon in your toolbar → Options, and paste the token. Now select text on any page to clip it.
Running your own May or Shall server instead? Put its address in the extension's Options. Questions? See the guide ↗
