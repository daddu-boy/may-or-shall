May or Shall

You're one step from clipping.

The clipper saves highlights into the free May or Shall app, which runs on your own computer, so your case data never leaves your machine. Download and open it once, and this extension connects to it automatically.

  1. Download and open the app (first launch: right-click → Open on Mac, or “More info → Run anyway” on Windows).
  2. Come back here. This extension finds the running app on its own, no setup.
  3. Select text on any page — a judgment, an order, a news report — and save it as a typed, source-linked card in your matter.
Already have the app running? Just click the May or Shall icon in your toolbar.
